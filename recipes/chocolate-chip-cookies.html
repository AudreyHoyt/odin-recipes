Best Ever Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 1 Cup Butter Room Temperature
  • 1/2 Cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup brown Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tsp vanilla
  • 3C flour
  • 1/2tsp baking powder
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 1tsp coarse kosher salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips or chocolate chunks
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Cream butter and white sugar together until fluffy
  3. Add in brown sugar and mix until fully combined
  4. mix in eggs and vanilla
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the rest of the ingredients (flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt)
  6. Combine dry ingredients with wet ingrients in thirds
  7. When the final third is partially mixed in and the texture is still lumpy and crumbly, add in chocolate chips and mix until just combined
  8. Scoop cookie dough into 1-2TBS sized balls onto a parchment lined cookie sheet
  9. Cook for 11-13 minutes, until edges are just set and the middle doesn't looked fully cooked
