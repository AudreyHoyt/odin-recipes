Overnight Coldbrew Coffee
  • Ground coffee
  • Water
  • Handfull of ice cubes
  • Large jar or pitcher
  • Something to cover the jar or pitcher
  1. Pour about 1 cup of coffee grounds for every 4 cups of water into your vessel
  2. Toss in a handful of ice cubes
  3. Fill your vessel to the top with water
  4. Cover and leave on the counter overnight
  5. When you're ready to use, you can strain through cheese cloth, coffee filters, or a fine mesh seive
